SU23 CS6460 Project: AI Advisor for Major Selection

Team Member: Hongyu Qiao, Ningrui Zhou

Overview

The purpose of this project is to provide assistance in college major selection for high school graduates. We have developed a chatbot based on Dialogflow CX and ChatGPT, aiming to combine the advantages of vast information from web search and the scientific decision-making and natural conversation of human advisors, to help high school graduates gain a better understanding of college majors and make more informed decisions. The conversation flow of the chatbot is depicted in the following diagram.

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Click on the icon at the bottom right corner to open the chatbox.
  2. Step 2: Say something like 'Hi' to trigger the chatbot.
  3. Step 3: You could ask about any subject of your interest, e.g., 'tell me something about Computer Science' or 'Computer Science'.
  4. Step 4: You could ask about any factor to get further information, e.g., 'tell me more about the average salary' or 'average salary'.
  5. Step 5: You could tell the chatbot you are still undecided on major selection, e.g., 'I feel still undecided' or 'undecided'.
  6. Step 6: Answer the questions subsequently to obtain more detailed recommendations.
